 ===========================================================================================
					-DESCRIPTION-
 ===========================================================================================

			A program to edit the header in Advance-ROMs.

 ===========================================================================================
				       -ROMs SUPPORTED-
 ===========================================================================================
		
			    All Advance ROMs you may ever have :P		

 ===========================================================================================
				       -INSTRUCTIONS-
 ===========================================================================================

 1) Open your favourite ROM.
 2) Click the "Browse ROM folder..." and choose a folder.
 3) In the list, you'll see all the ROMs inside.
 4) Choose one of them and edit the header as you like.
 5) Click the "Save" button when you're done.

Have fun ;)
